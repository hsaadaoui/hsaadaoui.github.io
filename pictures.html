About

I am very passionate about traveling and going to different places not only for leisure but to explore and see the beauty of our planet and its diversity. It is mesmerizing to see how people live their everyday lives, learn about their struggles and aspirations, experience their culture, listen to their music, etc. It gives me a bigger perspective about life and makes me appreciate every moment of it. I have always came back home from my travels with an even stronger urge to travel more. As times passes by and we grow older, we don't remember time but we remember memories. Here I would like to share with you some of the pictures I have taken, and tell you my story of why I travel. More pictures and stories from travels to so many other countries will be added here.
