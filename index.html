About

I am a Physicist and data scientist, and hold a PhD in Physics from the University of British Columbia (Vancouver, Canada). I have worked on various research projects at TRIUMF laboratory in Canada, and Paul Scherrer Institute (PSI) in Switzerland.

I have published more than 60 peer-reviewed articles in leading scientific journals, and 10 of these are 1st-author publications. I have conceived, proposed, led, and completed sucessfuly 6+ projects at TRIUMF ad PSI.

My professional interests are in Science, Data science, Technology, Programming, and Finance. I like to tel a good story with data, and dig deep to find patterns and correlations.
