About

Hassan Saadaoui is a Physicist, data scientist, and holds a PhD in Physics from the University of British Columbia. He has worked on various research projects at TRIUMF laboratory in Canada, and Paul Scherrer Institute in Switzerland.

Hassan's professional interests are in Science, Technology, Data science, Programming, and Finance.

Hassan speaks English, French, Tamazikht, Arabic and some German. He also speaks fluently to computers in Python, C++, R, Matlab, HTML. For more info write to hn.saadaoui^at^gmail^dot^com.
