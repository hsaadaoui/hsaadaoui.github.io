About

I am a Physicist and Data Scientist, and hold a PhD in Physics from the University of British Columbia (Vancouver, Canada). I have worked on various industry projects in the Silicon Valley (California); and academic projects at TRIUMF National laboratory in Canada, and Paul Scherrer Institute (PSI) in Switzerland.

I have published more than 70 peer-reviewed articles in leading scientific journals such as Nature communications, Physical Review Letters, Nano Letters and others. I have conceived, proposed, led, and completed sucessfuly 10+ projects at TRIUMF and PSI, resulting in 10 first-author publications.

My professional interests are in Data Science, Information Technology, Physics, and Finance.
